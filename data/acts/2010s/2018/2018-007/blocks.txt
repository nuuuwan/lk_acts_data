L.D.O. 37/2014

AN ACT TO AMEND THE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTYACT, NO. 36 OF 2003

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Intellectual Property(Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2018.

2.Section 161 of the Intellectual Property Act, No. 36 of2003 is hereby amended by the insertion immediately aftersubsection (4) thereof, the following new subsection:

(4A)The Minister may prescribe any geographicalindication in respect of any goods or products forthe purpose of this Act..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
