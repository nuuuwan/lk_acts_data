AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL DEFENCE FUNDACT, NO. 9 OF 1985

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the National Defence Fund(Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2018.

2.Section 3 of National Defence Fund Act, No. 9 of 1985(hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) is herebyamended as follows:

(1)by the repeal of paragraphs (a) and (b) of subsection(2) of that section and substitution therefor of thefollowing paragraph:

(a) the following ex-officio members:

(i)the Secretary to the President of theRepublic;

(ii)the Secretary to the Ministry of theMinister assigned the subject ofDefence; and ;

(2)by relettering the paragraph (c) of subsection (2) ofthat section as paragraph (b); and

(3)by the substitution in subsections (3) and (4), forthe words and figures paragraph (c) of subsection(2), of the words and figures paragraph (b) ofsubsection (2).

Short title.
