L.D.O. 63/2016

AN ACT TO AMEND THE EXCISE ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 52)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Excise (Amendment)Act, No. 4 of 2018 and shall come into operation onApril 1, 2018.

2.Section 15 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) ishereby amended by the repeal of paragraphs (b) and (c) ofthat section, and the substitution therefor of the following:

(b)no tree producing toddy, other than kithul tree shallbe tapped;

(c)no toddy shall be drawn or lowered from any treeother than the kithul tree..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
