AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRESCRIPTION ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 68)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Prescription(Amendment) Act, No. 26 of 2014.

2.Section 15 of the Prescription Ordinance, is herebyrepealed and the following section is substituted therefor:

15. Nothing herein contained shall in anyway

(a)affect the alienable or inalienablerights of the State; or

(b)apply to any proceedings in anyaction for divorce, or to any case inwhich special provision has been ormay hereafter be made for regulatingand determining the period withinwhich actions may be commencedagainst any public officer or otherperson..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

ThisOrdinancenot to affectState orcausesmatrimonial.

Sinhala text toprevail in caseof inconsistency.
