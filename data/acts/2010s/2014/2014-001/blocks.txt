AN ACT TO AMEND THE INSTITUTE OF POLICY STUDIES OF SRI LANKAACT, NO. 53 OF 1988

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Institute of PolicyStudies of Sri Lanka (Amendment) Act, No. 1 of 2014.

2.Section 7 of the Institute of Policy Studies ofSri Lanka Act, No. 53 of 1988 is hereby amended by therepeal of paragraph (c) of that section, and the substitutiontherefor of the following paragraph:

(c)the Governor of the Central Bank of Sri Lanka or anofficer nominated by the Governor;.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
