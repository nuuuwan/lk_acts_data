L.D.O. 3/2014

AN ACT TO AMEND THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES FILLING OF VACANCIES(SPECIAL PROVISIONS) ACT, NO. 30 OF 2013

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Local Authorities Fillingof Vacancies (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 37of 2014 and shall come into operation on such date as theMinister may appoint by Order published in the Gazette.

2.Section 2 of the Local Authorities Filling ofVacancies (Special Provisions) Act, No. 30 of 2013 is herebyamended by the repeal of the proviso to that section.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between theSinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, Sinhala text shall prevail.
