AN ACT TO AMEND THE MUNICIPAL COUNCILS ORDINANCE(CHAPTER 252)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Municipal Councils(Amendment) Act, No. 34 of 2014 and shall come intooperation on such date as the Minister may appoint by OrderPublished in the Gazette.

2.The following new section is hereby insertedimmediately after section 215A of the Municipal CouncilsOrdinance (Chapter 252) and shall have effect as section215B of that Ordinance :

215B. The provisions of the proviso tosection 215A of the Municipal CouncilsOrdinance as amended by the LocalAuthorities (Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of2012 shall not be applicable to any budget orsupplementary budget to be submitted to aMunicipal Council constituted in terms of theresults of elections held prior to the date ofcoming into operation of Local Authorities(Special Provisions) Act, No. 21 of 2012 andLocal Authorities Elections (Amendment) Act,No. 22 of 2012 respectively..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
