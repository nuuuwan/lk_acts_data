L.D.O. 35/2008

AN ACT TO AMEND THE PROVINCIAL COUNCILS ACT, NO. 42 OF 1987

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Provincial Councils(Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 2010.

2.Section 25 of the Provincial Councils Act, No. 42 of1987 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that section bythe substitution for the words three months before thecommencement of such financial year, of the words onemonth before the commencement of such financial year.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
