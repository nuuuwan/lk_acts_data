AN ACT TO AMEND THE JUDICATURE ACT, NO. 2 OF 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment)Act, No. 26 of 2017.

2.Section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 is herebyamended by the repeal of paragraph (a) of that section andthe substitution therefor of the following paragraph:

(a) not less than ten and not more than one hundredand ten judges, each of whom shall be known as aJudge of the High Court;.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
