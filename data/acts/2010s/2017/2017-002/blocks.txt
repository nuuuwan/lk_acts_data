AN ACT TO AMEND THE DIVINEGUMAACT, NO. 1 OF 2013

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Divineguma(Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2017.

2.The Long title to the Divineguma Act, No. 1 of 2013(hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) is herebyamended as follows:

(1)by the substitution for the expression DEPARTMENTOF DIVINEGUMA DEVELOPMENT of theexpression DEPARTMENT OF SAMURDHIDEVELOPMENT; and
