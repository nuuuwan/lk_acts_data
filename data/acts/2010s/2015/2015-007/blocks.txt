AN ACT TO AMEND THE IMMIGRANTS AND EMIGRANTSACT (CHAPTER 351)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Immigrants and Emigrants(Amendment) Act, No. 7 of 2015.

2.The Immigrants and Emigrants Act (Chapter 351) ishereby amended by the insertion, immediately after section35 thereof, of the following new section:

35A. The Authority prescribed to issue orrenew passports may take finger printing forthe inclusion in the passport, from a personapplying for a passport or the renewal of apassport..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
