AN ACT TO AMEND THE BETTING AND GAMING LEVY ACT,NO. 40 OF 1988

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1. This Act may be cited as the Betting and Gaming Levy(Amendment) Act, No. 14 of 2015 and shall be deemed forall purposes, to have come into operation on January 1,2015.

2. Section 2 of the Betting and Gaming Levy Act, No. 40of 1988 (hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment),as last amended by Act, No. 19 of 2013 is hereby furtheramended as follows:-

(1)in subsection (1A) of that section, by thesubstitution for the words a levy at the rate of fiveper centum of the following words:-
