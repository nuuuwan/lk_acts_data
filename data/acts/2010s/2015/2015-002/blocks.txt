AN ACT TO AMEND THE FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCESACT, NO. 2 OF 1996

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Fisheries and AquaticResources (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 2015.

2.Section 49 of the Fisherise and Aquatic ResourcesAct, No. 2 of 1996 (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment) is hereby amended by the repeal of subsection(2A) of that section and the substitution therefor of thefollowing subsection:

(2A) Any person who contravenes or fails tocomply with the provisions of sections 14A or 14F ofthis Act shall commit an offence and shall onconviction after summary trial before a Magistrate beliable to an imprisonment for a term not exceedingtwo years or to a fine not less than the amountsspecified in Column II of the Schedule hereto whichshall be determined by taking into consideration thelength of the fishing boat specified in correspondingentry in the Column I of the Schedule:
