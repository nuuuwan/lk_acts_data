AN ACT TO AMEND THE REGISTRATION OF DOCUMENTS ORDINANCE(CHAPTER 117)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Registration ofDocuments (Amendment) Act, No. 21 of 2013 andshall be deemed to have come into operation onJanuary 1, 2013.
