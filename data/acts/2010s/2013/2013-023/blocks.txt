AN ACT TO AMEND THE KANDYAN MARRIAGE ANDDIVORCE ACT (CHAPTER 113)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1. This Act may be cited as the Kandyan Marriage andDivorce (Amendment) Act, No. 23 of 2013 and shall be deemedto have come into operation on January 1, 2013.
