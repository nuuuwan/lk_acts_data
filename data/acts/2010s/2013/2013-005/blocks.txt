AN ACT TO AMEND THE PORTS AND AIRPORTS DEVELOPMENT LEVYACT, NO. 18 OF 2011.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1.This Act may be cited as the Ports and AirportsDevelopment Levy (Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 2013.

2.Section 3 of the Ports and Airports Development LevyAct, No. 18 of 2011 (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment) is hereby amended in subsection (2), by therepeal of paragraphs (a) and (b) of that section and thesubstitution therefor of the following new paragraphs:-

(a) for the purpose of processing and re-export;

(b) to be used as a raw material for the manufacture ofgoods for exports; or

(c) for storage, fabrication, repairing, servicing orexportation as the case may be, being equipment,tools, materials, consumables in or from a bondedfacility to be used in the petroleum operationsconducted within or outside Sri Lanka.

For the purposes of paragraph (c), the expressionpetroleum operations shall have the meaning assignedto it under the Petroleum Resources Act, No. 26 of 2003..

3.Section 16 of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed and the following new section is substitutedtherefor:-

Amendment tosection 3 of thePorts andAirportsDevelopmentLevy Act, No. 18of 2011.

Short title.

Replacement ofsection 16 of theprincipalenactment.
