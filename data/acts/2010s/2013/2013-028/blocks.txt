AN ACT TO REPEAL THE PARLIAMENTARY SCHOLARSHIP BOARD ACT,NO. 22 OF 2002 ; AND TO PROVIDE FOR MATTERS CONNECTED

THEREWITH OR INCIDENTAL THERETO

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the ParliamentaryScholarship Board (Repeal) Act, No. 28 of 2013.

2.The Parliamentary Scholarship Board Act, No. 22 of2002 is hereby repealed.

3.(1) All moneys lying, on the day immediatelypreceding the date of commencement of this Act, to the creditof the Fund of the Parliamentary Scholarship Board, shallwith effect from the date of coming into operation of this Act,stand transferred to the Consolidated Fund.

(2) Any property, movable or immovable which has beenvested in or purchased or acquired by or leased to or placedat the disposal of the Parliamentary Scholarship Board or inthe possession or control of such Board and subsisting on theday immediately preceding the date of coming into operationof this Act shall, subject to any terms and conditions attachingto such property, be deemed, with effect from the date ofcommencement of this Act, to have been purchased oracquired by or leased to or placed at the disposal of or in thepossession or control of the Parliament.

4.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
