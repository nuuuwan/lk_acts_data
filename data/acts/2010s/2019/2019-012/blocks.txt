AN ACT TO REPEAL THE REVIVAL OF UNDERPERFORMINGENTERPRISES OR UNDERUTILIZED ASSETS ACT, NO. 43 OF 2011

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Revival ofUnderperforming Enterprises or Underutilized Assets(Repeal) Act, No. 12 of 2019.

2.The Revival of Underperforming Enterprises orUnderutilized Assets Act, No. 43 of 2011 (hereinafter referredto as the repealed Act) is hereby repealed.

3.For the avoidance of doubt it is hereby declared that,with effect from the date of commencement of this Act, theUnderperforming Enterprises or Underutilized Assets,specified in Schedule I and Schedule II of the repealed Actvested in the Secretary to the Treasury for and on behalf ofthe State under section 2 of the repealed Act, and subsistingon the day immediately preceding the date of commencementof this Act, shall continue to be so vested in the Secretary tothe Treasury for and on behalf of the State.
