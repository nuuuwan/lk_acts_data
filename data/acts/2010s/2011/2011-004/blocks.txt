AN ACT TO AMEND THE MEDIATION BOARDS ACT, NO. 72 OF 1988

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Mediation Boards(Amendment) Act, No. 4 of 2011.

2.Section 7 of the Mediation Boards Act, No. 72 of1988 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that section asfollows:

(1)by the substitution in paragraph (a) of thatsubsection for the words twenty five thousandrupees in value of the words two hundred andfifty thousand rupees in value;;

(2)by the substitution in paragraph (c) of thatsubsection for the words Second Schedule to thisAct, of the words Second Schedule to this Act;or; and

(3)by the insertion immediately after paragraph (c) ofthat subsection, of the following paragraph:

(d)the offence under section 367 or 368B of thePenal Code is committed by a person belowthe age of eighteen years, in respect of anyproperty, the value of which does not exceedrupees five thousand,.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
