1Excise (Amendment) Act, No. 20 of 2011

[Certified on 31st March, 2011]

L.D.O. 3/2011.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE EXCISE ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 52)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.(1) This Act may be cited as the Excise (Amendment)Act, No. 20 of 2011.

(2) The provisions of this Act shall for all purposes bedeemed to have come into operation on November 19, 2010.

2.Section 15 of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52) ishereby amended by the repeal of paragraphs (b) and (c) ofthat section, and the substitution therefor of thefollowing :

(b)no tree producing toddy, other than the kithul treeand the palmyrah tree, shall be tapped ; and

(c)no toddy shall be drawn from any tree, other thanthe kithul tree and the palmayrah tree..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
