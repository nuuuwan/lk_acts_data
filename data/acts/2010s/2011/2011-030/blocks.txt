AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRADESHIYA SANWARDANA BANKACT, NO. 41 OF 2008

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1.This Act may be cited as the Pradeshiya SanwardanaBank (Amendment) Act, No. 30 of 2011.

2.Section 4 of the Pradeshiya Sanwardana Bank Act,No. 41 of 2008 is hereby repealed and the following Sectionsubstituted therefor :

4. The head office of the Bank may beestablished in any location in Sri Lanka,outside the Administrative District ofColombo, as may be determined by theBoard..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
