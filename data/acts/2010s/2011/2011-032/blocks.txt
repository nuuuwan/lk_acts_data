AN ACT TO AMEND THE NAVY ACT (CHAPTER 358)

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This act may be cited as the Navy (Amendment) Act,No. 32 of 2011.

2.Section 28 of the Navy Act (Chapter 358) is herebyamended by the repeal of subsection (1) of that section andthe substitution of the following subsection therefor :

28.(1) The Commander of the Navy may

(a) where it is in relation to an offence,other than an offence which is expresslyrequired by this Act to be tried by acourt martial;

(b) where an officer of the rank ofLieutenant Commander or belowcommits and is to be charged with anon-capital offence; and

(c) if in his opinion, the nature of theoffence committed does not warrant acourt martial,

authorise an officer not below the rank of aCaptain, to summarily try the accused:

Provided that prior to the commencement of thetrial, the officer authorised to conduct the trialshall ask the accused whether he chooses

(i) to be tried by a court martial ; or

(ii) to be tried summarily.
