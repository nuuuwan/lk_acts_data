An AcT TO AMEND THE HOLIDAYS ORDINANCE.
re [Date of Assent: December 21, 1957] :

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty,
by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and
the House of Representatives of Ceylon in this present
Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the
same, as follows: —

1. This Act may be cited as the Holidays (Amend-
ment) Act, No. 57 of 1957, and shall come into
operation on January 1, 1958.

2. Section 6 of the Holidays Ordinance (herein-
after referred to as the ‘‘ principal enactment ’’) is
hereby repealed.

3. The following new Schedules are hereby substi-
tuted for the First and Second Schedules to the
principal enactment : —
