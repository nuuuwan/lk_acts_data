Act, No. 32 of 1957 ;

An ACT TO AMEND THE Civit PRocepureE Cops.
[Date of Assent: May 2, 1957]

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty,
by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and
the House of: Representatives of Ceylon in this present
Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the
same, as follows: —

1. This Act may be cited as the Civil Procedure
Code (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 1957.

2. Section 286 of the Civil Procedure Code is
hereby amended, in the proviso to that section, by the
substitution, for the words ‘‘ there shall be annexed
of the words “‘ there shall, if the purchaser so requires

but not otherwise, be annexed ’’.
