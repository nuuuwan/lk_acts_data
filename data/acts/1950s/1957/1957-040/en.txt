AN Act TO AMEND THE EstTaTE Duty ORDINANCE.

[Date of Assent: September 6, 1957]

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent
Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the
Senate and the House of Representatives of Ceylon
in this present Parliament assembled, and by the
authority of the same, as follows: —

4. This Act may be cited as the Estate Duty
(Amendment) Act, No. 40 of 1957.

2. Section 2 of the Estate Duty Ordinance (here-
after in this Act referred to as ‘‘ the principal
enactment ’’), as amended by Act No. 25 of 1948, is
hereby further amended as follows :—

(1) in sub-section (1) of that section, by the
substitution, for the words ‘“‘a Deputy
Commissioner ’’, of the words ‘*‘ Deputy

ee Commissioners ”’;

(2) in sub-section (2) of that section, by the
substitution, for the words ‘the
Commissioners ’’, of the words “the
Commissioner ’’; and

(3) in the marginal note to that section, by the
substitution, for the word ‘‘ Commis-
sioner,’’, of the words ‘*‘ Commissioner,
Deputy Commissioners,’’.

3. Section 77 of the principal enactment, as
amended by Act No. 25 of 1948, is hereby further
amended in sub-section (1) of that section, in the
definition of the word ‘‘ Commissioner ’’, by the
Substitution, for the words ‘‘ the Deputy ’’, of the
words ‘‘ a Deputy ’’.
