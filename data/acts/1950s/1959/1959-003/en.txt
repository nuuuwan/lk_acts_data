Tea Research (Amendment) Act, 3
No. 3 of 1959

L. D.—O. 47/58.

An Act TO AMEND THE TEA RESEARCH ORDINANCE.

(Date of Assent: January 21, 1959}

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty,
by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and
the House of Representatives of Ceylon in this present
Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the
same, as follows: —

4, This Act may be cited as the Tea Research
(Amendment) Act, No. 3 of 1959.

2. Section 5 of the Tea Research Ordinance (here-

after in this Act referred to as the “‘ principal
enactment ’’), as amended by the Proclamation pub-
lished in Gazette Extraordinary No. 9,773 of Sep-
tember 24, 1947, and by Act No. 24 of 1948, Act
No. 51 of 1953 and Act No. 20 of 1955, is hereby
further amended, in paragraph (a) of sub-section (1)
of that section, by the addition, at the end of that
paragraph, of the following : — |

‘* The Director of the Tea Research Institute of
Ceylon.”’

8. Section 12 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended as follows :—

(a) by the renumbering of sub-section (2) of that
section as sub-section (3); and

(b) by the insertion, immediately after sub-section
(1) of that section, of the following new
sub-section : —

‘* (2) The Board shall have full power to
fix the rates of emoluments, bonus, royal-
ties and other remuneration payable to its
officers and servants, and to apportion
moneys derived from patents, brevets
d’invention and other similar rights and
privileges between itself and its officers and
servants in such manner as it may deem
equitable.’’.
