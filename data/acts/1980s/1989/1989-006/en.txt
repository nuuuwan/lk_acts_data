AN ACT TO AMEND THE TURNOVER Tax Act, No. 69 or 1981

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may he cited as the Turnover Tax (Amend-
ment) Act, No. 6 of 1989.

2. Section 46 of the Turnover Tax Act, No. 69 of 1961
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is
hereby amended in subsection (2) thereof, by the repeal of
paragraph (f) of that subsection, and the substitution of the
following paragraph therefor: —

“ (f) the turnover tax attributable to such transaction
and the withholding turnover tax deducted under
section 50a; and”.

3. The following new Chapter is hereby inserted imme-

diately after Chapter XV, and shall have effect as Chapter
XVA of the principal enactment:— |
