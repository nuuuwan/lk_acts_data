An AcT TO AMEND THE RECOVERY OF LOANS BY BANKS
(SPECIAL Provisions) Act, No. 4 or 1990.

B™ it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Recovery of Loans by
Banks (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 24
of 1995.

2. Section 22 of the Recovery of Loans by Banks
(Special Provisions) Act, No. 4 of 1990, is hereby amended,
by the substitution, for the definition of the word “bank”
of the following definition :—

*“ bank” means—

(a) a licensed commercial bank within the meaning of
the Banking Act, No. 30 of 1988, other than any
bank established under the provisions of the
Regional Rural Developrient Bank Act, No. 15 of
1985 ;

(b) the Development Finance Corporation of Ceylon
established by the Development Finance Corpora-
tion ef Ceylon Act (Chapter 165) ; and

(c) the National Savings Bank established by the
National Savings Bank Act, No. 30 of 1971.’.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
