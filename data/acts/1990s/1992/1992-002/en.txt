‘AN Act TO AMEND THE Sri LANKA Ports AvutHorrry ‘ACT,
No, 51 or 1979

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Ports
Authority (Amendment) Act, No. 2 of 1952.

2. Section 39 (1) of the Sri Lanka Ports Authority Act,
No. 51 of 1979, (as amended by Act No. 7 of 1984 and Act
No. 35 of 1984), is hereby further amended as follows :—

(a) by the substitution, for the words “sell by public
auction”, of the words “sell or dispose of by
public auction or by calling for tenders” ;

(b) in paragraph (b) thereof, by the substitution, for
the words “of the date of the sale or disposal and
his concurrence has been obtained for such sale
or disposal”, of the words “of the date of the
sale or disposal by public auction or the notice
calling for tenders and his concurrence has been
obtained for such sale or disposal by public auction
or notice calling for tenders”; and

(c) in paragraph (d) thereof, by the substitution, for
the words ‘if no bid is made at such sale,”, of the
words “if no bid is made at such sale or disposal
by such public auction or no tender is received
in response to the notice calling for tenders, ”.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and the Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text
*- shall prevail,
