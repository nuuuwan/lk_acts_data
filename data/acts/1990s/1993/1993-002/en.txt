AN ACT TO MAKE SUCH AMENDMENTS IN WRITTEN LAW AS
ARE CGNSEQUENTIAL TO THE CHANGE OF DESIGNATION OF
Grama Seva Nivapwarr AND GRAMA SEvA’ NILADHARI'S
DrvisIon. |

BE it enacted by the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri
Lanka as follows :— |

1. This Act may be cited as the Garma Seva Niladhari
(Change of Designation) Act, No. 2 of 1993.

2. With effect from the date of commencement of this
Act, in every context in which a Grama Seva Niladhari or
2 Grama Seva Niladhari’s Division is mentioned in any
written law: other than the Grama Sevaka (Change of
Designation) Law, No. 5 of 1977, or in any notice, communi-
cation. form or other document issued, made, required, or
authorised by or under any written law, there shall be
substituted. for the words ‘Grama Seva Niladhari” and
“Crama Seva Niladhari’s Division”, the words “Grama
Niladhari ”, and “Grama Niladhari’s Division ”, respectively.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
