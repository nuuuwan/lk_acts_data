Medical (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1993
[Certified on 17th March, 1993]

L. D.—O. 52/92
An AcT TO AMEND THE MEDICAL ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Medical (Amendment)
Act, No. 13 of 1993. -

2. Section 41 of the Medical Ordinance is hereby
amended as follows :— ;
(i) in paragraph (a) of subsection (2) of that section, by
the substitution, for the words “fifteen years;
and”, of the words “eight years; and”; and

(ii) in sub-paragraph (i) of paragraph (a) of subsection
(2B) of that section, by the substitution, for the
words “eight years”, of the words “four years”.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the
Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shal]

prevail.
