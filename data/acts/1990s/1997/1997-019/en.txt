AN ACT TO AMEND THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE ACT,
No. 15 oF 1979

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Code of Criminal
procedure (Amendment) Act, No. 19 of 1997.

2. Section 303 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act,
No. 15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “ principal
enactment ”) is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that
section, by the substitution for the words “ is a mandatory
sentence of imprisonment, ", of the words “ is a mandatory
minimum sentence of imprisonment, ”.

3. The First Schedule to the principal enactment is hereby
amended as follows :—

(1) by the omission, from Column 8 of that Schedule of
the words “ Magistrate’s Court ” appearing in the
entries in that Column, corresponding to the entries
in Column I of that Schedule, relating respectively,
to sections 286a, 308, 360a, 3608, 360c, 364, 3644
and 365s ;

(2) by the repeal of all the entries relating to section 365
and the substitution therefor of the following
entries :-—
