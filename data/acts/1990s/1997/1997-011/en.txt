AN ACT TO AMEND THE TOWER HALL THEATRE FOUNDATION
Act, No 1 oF 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist —

Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Tower Hall Theatre
Foundation (Amendment) Act, No. 11 of 1997.

2. Section 3 of the Tower Hall Theatre Foundation Act,
No. 1 of 1978, is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that
section by the repeal of paragraph (f) thereof and the
substitution therefor of the following paragraph:—

“(f) the Secretary tothe Ministry of the Minister in charge .

of the subject of Cultural Affairs;”.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
