AN ACT TO AMEND TITE CODE OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Act, No. 52 oF 1979,

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

I. This Act may be cited as the Code of Intellectual
Property (Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 1997.

2. With effect from the date of commencement of this
Act, wherever in any provision of the Code of Intellectual
Property Act, No. 52 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the
“principal enactment”) or any other written law or notice,
permit, communication, form or other document issued, made
required or authorized, by or under the principal enactment
or other written law—

(a) the expression “Registry of Patents and Trade Marks”
and “Registry” denoting the Registry of Patents
and Trade Marks occurs, there shall be substituted
the expression “National Intellectual Property
office of Sri Lanka” and “Office” respectively ;

(b) mention is madc to any designation set out inColumn
I of the Schedule to this Act, there shall be
substitutcd therefor, the designation set out in
corresponding entry in Column II of that
Schedule. .
