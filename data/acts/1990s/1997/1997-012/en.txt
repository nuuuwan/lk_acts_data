AN Act To AMEND THE MARRIAGE REGISTRATION ORDINANCE
ia

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Marriage Registration
(Amendment) Act, No. 12 of 1997.

2. Section 22 of the Marriage Registration Ordinance
(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is
hereby amended in paragraph (a) of subsection (1) thereof, by
the substitution for the words “under twenty-one years of
age ; ”, of the words “under eighteen years of age;”.

3. Section 24 of the principal enactment is hereby
amended in subsection (6) thereof, by the substitution, for
the words “value of ten rupees”, of the words “value of
twenty-five rupees”.

4. The Second Schedule to the principal enactment as
replaced by Law No. 41 of 1975, is hereby repealed, and the
following Schedule substituted therefor :-—

5. Inthe event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
