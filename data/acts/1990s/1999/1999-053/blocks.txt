AN ACT TO AMEND THE NAVY ACT

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Navy (Amendment) Act,No. 53 of 1999.

2. Section 28 of the Navy Act, as amended by Act, No. 11of 1993 is hereby amended in subsection (1) of that section, bythe substitution, for the words the rank of Lieutenant of thewords the rank of Lieutenant Commander.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act,the Sinhala text shall prevail.
