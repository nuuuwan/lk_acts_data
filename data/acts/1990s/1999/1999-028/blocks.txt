AN ACT TO AMEND THE OFFENCES AGAINST PUBLICPROPERTY ACT, NO. 12 OF 1982

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Offences Against PublicProperty (Amendment) Act, No. 28 of 1999.

2. Section 8 of the Offences Against Public Property Act,No. 12 of 1982 is hereby amended in the proviso to subsection(1) if that section, by the substitution, for the words exceedsfive thousand rupees of the words exceeds twenty fivethousand rupees.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act,the Sinhala text shall prevail.
