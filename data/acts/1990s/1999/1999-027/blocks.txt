AN ACT TO AMEND THE JUDICATURE ACT, NO.2 OF 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

1.This Act may be cited as the Judicature(Amendment) Act, No.27 of 1999.

2.Section 48 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978, ishereby amended, by the repeal of the proviso to that sectionand the substitution therefor, of the following proviso :-

Provided that where any criminal prosecution, proceeding or matter (except on an inquiry preliminary tocommittal for trial) is continued before the successor ofany such judge, the accused may demand that thewitnesses be resummoned and reheard..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between theSinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala Text shallprevail
