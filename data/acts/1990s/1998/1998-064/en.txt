‘AN ACT TO AMEND THE Wibows’ AND ORPHANS' PENSION
Funp OrpINANcE.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Widows’ and Orphans’
Pension Fund (Amendment) Act, No. 64 of 1998.

2. Section 29 of the Widows’ and Orphans’ Pension
Fund Ordinance is hereby amended by the insertion, atthe end
Cf that section, of the following proviso :—

“Provided further thata male orphan oran unmarried

female orphan, who is twenty one years or more of age.

shall, so long as he or she is unemployed be entitled to
the allowance or pension until he or she attains the age
of twenty-six years.”.

3. Inthe event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
