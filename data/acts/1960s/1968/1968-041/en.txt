Defence Regulations (Resuscitation)
Act, No. 41 of 1968

L. D.—O. 9/50.

AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE RESUSCITATION AND
CONTINUANCE IN FORCE OF THE DEFENCE REGULA-
TIONS (ExTENSION) AcT, No. 24 oF 1950, wHICH
EXPIRED AFTER DECEMBER 9, 1964, AND FOR THE
VALIDATION RENDERED NECESSARY BY SUCH EXPIRY.

{Date of Assent: November 24. 1968]

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty,
by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and
the House of Representatives of Ceylon in this present
Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the
same, as follows :—

4. This Act may be cited as the Defence
Regulations (Resuscitation) Act, No. 41 of 1968.

2. (1) The Defence Regulations (Extension) Act,
No. 24 of 1950, which, by virtue of a resolution passed
by the Senate and the House of Representatives under
section 6 of that Act, continued in force until December
9, 1964, and then expired, shall be deemed to have
been in force during the period commencing on the
date of such expiry and ending on the day immediately
preceding the date of the commencement of this Act
and to be in force thereafter until December 31, 1968,
as if the aforesaid resolution had declared that the
Defence Regulations (Extension) Act, No. 24 of 1950,
shall continue in force until December 31, 1968, and
may be continued in force for any further period or
periods in the manner prescribed in section 6 of that
Act.

(2) All such orders and instruments as were made
under any of the Defence Regulations specified in the
Defence Regulations (Extension) Act, No. 24 of 1950,
and were in force at the time of the expiry of that Act
shall be deemed to have been in force during the period
commencing on the date of expiry of that Act and
ending on the day immediately preceding the date of
the commencement of this Act and to be in force there-
after until thev are rescinded or annulled or until they
expire.
