Motor Traffic (Amendment) Act, No. 20 of 1968
L. D.—O. 22/66.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE Motor TRAFFIC ACT.
[Date of Assent: June 11, 1968]

BE it enacted by the Queen’s Most Excellent Majesty,
by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and
the House of Representatives of Ceylon in this
present Parliament assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows: —

1. This Act may be cited as the Motor Traffic
(Amendment) Act, No. 20 of 1968.

2. The Motor Traffic Act is hereby amended by
the insertion, immediately after section 156, of the
following new section which shall have effect as section
1564 of that Act:—

* Riding in 156a. No conductor of an omnibus on

SEAS a highway shall permit any passenger
to ride, and no passenger shall ride, in
any omnibus except in a seat provided
for the use of passengers or in the
gangway provided between two rows of
seats in a single-decked omnibus, or in
the gangway provided between two rows
of seats in the lower deck of a double-
decked omnibus. ”’
