AN ACT TO AMEND THE WIDOWS AND ORPHANS PENSION SCHEME(ARMED FORCES) ACT, NO. 18 OF 1970

BE enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Widows and OrphansPension Scheme (Armed Forces) (Amendment) Act, No. 29of 2009.

2.The Widows and Orphans Pension Scheme (ArmedForces) Act, No. 18 of 1970 (hereinafter referred to as theprincipal enactment) is hereby amended by the insertionimmediately after section 3A thereof, of the following newsection:

3AA.For the purposes of sections 3, 3A and3B of this Act, a member of the armed forcesmeans any person

(a)who, being a male, is an officer or soldierof the Regular Force of the Sri LankaArmy; or

(b)who, being a male is an officer or seamanof the Regular Naval Force of the RoyalCeylon Navy or Sri Lanka Navy as thecase may be; or

(c)who, being a male, is an officer or airmanof the Regular Air Force of the RoyalCeylon Air Force or Sri Lanka Air Forceas the case may be..
