AN ACT TO AMEND THE BOARD OF INVESTMENT OF SRI LANKA LAW,NO. 4 OF 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Board of Investment ofSri Lanka (Amendment) Act, No. 36 of 2009.

2.Schedule B to the Board of Investment of Sri LankaLaw, No. 4 of 1978 (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment), is hereby amended by the insertion, immediatelyafter the item The National Film Corporation of Sri Lanka Act,No. 47 of 1971, of the following new item :

The Inland Revenue Act, No. 10 of 2006.

3.The amendment made in Schedule B to the principalenactment by section 2 shall for all purposes be deemed tohave come into force on March 31, 2006, being the date onwhich the Inland Revenue Act, No. 10 of 2006 came intoforce.

4.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Short title.

Amendment ofSchedule B tothe principalenactment.

Amendment toSchedule B to bedeemed to havecome in to forceon the date ofcommencementof the InlandRevenue Act.

Sinhala text toprevail in caseof inconsistency.
