AN ACT TO AMEND THE SPORTS LAW, NO. 25 OF 1973

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

1. This Act may be cited as the Sports (Amendment) Act, No. 33 of 2005.

Short title.

Amendment of section 18 of the principal enactment.

2. Section 18 of the Sports Law, No. 25 of 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph (b) of subseciton (2) of that section and the substitution therefor, of the following paragraph :

(b) members, if any of the International Olympic Committee who are Sri Lankan Nationals, who shall be ex-officio members..

3. Section 18A of the principal enactment is hereby replaced and, the following section substituted therefor :

Replacement of section 18A of the principal enactment.

Term of office of members of Committee.

18A. Every member of such Committee, other than the ex-officio members, shall hold office for a term of four years..

4. Section 21 of the principal enactment is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph (e) of that section.

Amendment of section 21of the principal enactment.

5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala And Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of any inconsistency.
