AN ACT TO REPEAL THE SRI LANKA RAILWAYS AUTHORITY ACT, NO. 60

OF 1993; AND TO PROVIDE FOR MATTERS CONNECTED THEREWITH OR

INCIDENTAL THERTO

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka RailwaysAuthority (Repeal) Act, No. 3 of 2005.

2.The Sri Lanka Railways Authority Act, No. 60 of 1993,is hereby repealed.

3.(1) Notwithstanding the repeal of the Sri LankaRailways Authority Act, No. 60 of 1993 by section 2, allmoneys lying, on the date of commencement of this Act, tothe credit of the Sri Lanka Railways Authority established bythe Sri Lanka Railways Authority Act, No. 60 of 1993 shall,with effect from such date, stand transferred to theConsolidated Fund.

(2) (a) All proceedings instituted in any court or tribunalby or against the Sri Lanka Railways Authority establishedunder the repealed Act and pending on the day preceding thedate of commencement of this Act, shall be deemed, witheffect from the date of commencement of this Act, to beproceedings instituted by or against the Railways Departmentand be proceeded with accordingly.

(b) All judgments, decrees or orders entered by any courtor tribunal, in favour of, or against, the Sri Lanka RailwaysAuthority shall be deemed with effect from the date ofcommencement of this Act, to be judgments, decrees or ordersentered in favour of, or against the Railways Department andshall be enforced accordingly.
