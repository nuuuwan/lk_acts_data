AN ACT TO AMEND THE PRISONS ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Prisons (Amendment)Act, No. 22 of 2005.

2.Section 79 of the Prisons Ordinance (hereinafterreferred to as the principal enactment) is hereby amendedas follows :

(i)by the repeal of paragraph (g) of that section;

(ii)by the re-lettering of paragraph (h) of that section asparagraph (g).

3. Section 81 of the principal enactment is herebyamended as follows :

(1)in subsection (4) of that section

(a)by the repeal of paragraph (d) thereof ; and

(b)by the relettering of paragraph (e) thereofas paragraph (d) ;

(2)by the repeal of subsection (5) and (6) of thatsection ; and

(3)by the renumbering of subsections (7) and (8) assubsections (5) and (6) of that section.

4. Section 86 of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed.

5. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
