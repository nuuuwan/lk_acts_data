AN ACT TO AMEND THE WORKMENS COMPENSATION ORDINANCE.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the WorkmensCompensation (Amendment) Act, No. 10 of 2005.

2.Section 6 of the Workmens Compensation Ordinance(Chapter 139) (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment) is hereby amended in paragraph (D) ofsubsection (1) of that section by the omission of the wordsafter the expiry of a waiting period of three days,.

3.Section 11 of the principal enactment is herebyamended as follows :

(i)in the proviso to subsection (1) thereof, by thesubstitution for the words not exceeding anaggregate of one thousand rupees, of the wordsnot exceeding an aggregate of ten thousandrupees,; and

(ii)in subsection (2) thereof, by the substitution forthe words not less than one hundred rupees of thewords not less than one thousand rupees.

4.Section 12 of the principal enactment is herebyamended in subsection (1) of that section, by the repeal ofparagraphs (i), (ii), (iii) of that subsection and the substitutiontherefor of the following paragraphs :

(i)an amount not exceeding ten thousand rupees,where the compensation does not exceed twohundred thousand rupees ;

(ii)an amount not exceeding fifteen thousand rupees,where the compensation does not exceed threehundred thousand rupees,;
