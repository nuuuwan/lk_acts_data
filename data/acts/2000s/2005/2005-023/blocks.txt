AN ACT TO REPEAL THE CORPORAL PUNISHMENT ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Corporal Punishment(Repeal) Act, No. 23 of 2005.

2.The Corporal Punishment Ordinance (Chapter 21) ishereby repealed.

3. Wherever in any written law, there is included as partof a sentence to be imposed by Court in respect of any offence,a sentence of whipping, such law shall from and after the dateof the coming into operation of this Act, be deemed to beamended by the omission therefrom of such part of suchsentence as relates to whipping.

4. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
