[Certified on 13th February , 2006]

L.D.O. 35/2005.

AN ACT TO CHANGE THE DESIGNATION OF THE DIRECTOR OF MERCHANTSHIPPING AND TO MAKE SUCH AMENDMENTS IN WRITTEN LAW AS ARE CONSEQUENTIAL.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Merchant Shipping(Change of Designation) Act, No. 3 of 2006.

2.With effect from the date of commencement of thisAct, in every context in which Director of Merchantshipping appears in

(a) subsection (1) of section 3 and elsewhere inthe Merchant Shipping Act, No. 52 of 1971and in any other written law; and

(b) any notice, communication, or otherdocument issued, made, required orauthorized by or under any written law,

there shall be substituted for the words Director of MerchantShipping, the words Director-General of MerchantShipping.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Short title.

Amendment tosection 3 of theMerchantShipping Act, No.52 of 1971.

Sinhala text toprevail in case ofinconsistency.
