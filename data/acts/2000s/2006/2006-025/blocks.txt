AN ACT TO AMEND THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF ADVANCEDTECHNICAL EDUCATION ACT, NO. 29 OF 1995

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :-

1.This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Institute ofAdvanced Technical Education (Amendment) Act, No. 25of 2006.

2.The Long Title to the Sri Lanka Institute of AdvancedTechnical Education Act, No. 29 of 1995 (hereinafter referredto as the principal enactment) is hereby amended, by thesubstitution for the words AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THEESTABLISHMENT OF THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OFADVANCED TECHNICAL EDUCATION; of the wordsAN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE, ESTABLISHMENTOF THE SRI LANKA INSTITUTE OF ADVANCEDTECHNOLOGICAL EDUCATION;.

3.(1) In the principal enactment and in any other writtenlaw, there shall be substituted

(a)for the words Sri Lanka Institute of AdvancedTechnical Education of the words Sri LankaInstitute of Advanced Technological Education;

(b)for the words Advanced Technical Institute orAdvanced Technical Institutes of the wordsAdvanced Technological Institute or AdvancedTechnological Institutes as the case may be.

(2) Every reference to the Sri Lanka Institute of AdvancedTechnical Education and Advanced Technical InstituteorAdvanced Technical Institutes in any notice, notificationcontract, communication or other document shall be readand construed as a reference respectively to the Sri Lanka
