AN ACT TO AMEND THE MARRIAGE REGISTRATION ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Marriage Registration (Amendment) Act, No. 36 of 2006.

Short title.

2. The Marriage Registration Ordinance (Chapter 112) is hereby amended by the repeal of the Second Schedule thereto, (as repealed and replaced by Act, No. 12 of 1997) and the substitution therefor of the following Schedule:

Replacement of Second Schedule to Chapter 112.
