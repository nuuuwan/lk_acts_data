L.D.—O. 13/2002.

AN ACT TO AMEND THE Sri LANKA Press CounciL LAW

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Sri Lanka Press Council
(Amendment) Act, No. 13 of 2002.

2. Section 15 of the Sri Lanka Press Council Law, No. 5
of 1973 is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph () of
subsection (1) of that section.

3. Inthe event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
