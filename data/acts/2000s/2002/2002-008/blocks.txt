L.D.O. 56/2001.

AN ACT TO ABOLISH THE CHARGING OF SAVE THE NATIONCONTRIBUTION, UNDER THE SAVE THE NATION CONTRIBUTIONACT, NO. 5 OF 1996, FROM PERSONS TO WHOM THAT ACT APPLIES,

WITH EFFECT FROM OCTOBER 1, 2001

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Save the NationContribution (Abolition) Act, No. 8 of 2002.

2.Notwithstanding anything in the Save the NationContribution Act, No. 5 of 1996

(a)the Save the Nation Contribution shall not be chargedand recovered from any person to whom that Actapplies, for any quarter of any year ; and

(b)the employer of any person to whom that Act appliesshall not be required to deduct any amount that suchemployer is required to deduct from the emolumentspayable to such person, under section 4 of that Act,for any month,

commencing on or after October 1, 2001.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
