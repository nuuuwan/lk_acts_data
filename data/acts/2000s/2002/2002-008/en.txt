L.D.O. 56/2001.

AN ACT TO ABOLISH THE CHARGING OF SAVE THE NATION CONTRIBUTION, UNDER THE SAVE THE NATION CONTRIBUTION ACT, NO. 5 OF 1996, FROM PERSONS TO WHOM THAT ACT APPLIES,

WITH EFFECT FROM OCTOBER 1, 2001

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Save the Nation Contribution (Abolition) Act, No. 8 of 2002.

Short title.

2. Notwithstanding anything in the Save the Nation Contribution Act, No. 5 of 1996

Abolition of the charging of the Save the Nation Contribution with effect from October 1, 2001.

(a) the Save the Nation Contribution shall not be charged and recovered from any person to whom that Act applies, for any quarter of any year ; and

(b) the employer of any person to whom that Act applies shall not be required to deduct any amount that such employer is required to deduct from the emoluments payable to such person, under section 4 of that Act, for any month,

commencing on or after October 1, 2001.

3. In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.

Sinhala text to prevail in case of inconsistency.
