AN ACT TO AMEND THE PENAL CODE

BE it hereby enacted by the Parliament of the DemocraticSocialist Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Penal Code (Amendment)Act, No. 12 of 2002.

PART I

2.The Penal Code (hereinafter referred to as theprincipal enactment) is hereby amended by the repeal ofsection 118.

3.Chapter XIX of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed.

PART II

4.The Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15 of 1979is amended by the repeal of paragraph (f) of subsection (1) ofsection 135.

5.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand the Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.Sinhala text toprevail in case ofinconsistency.
