AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 5 OF 2005

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment)Act, No. 45 of 2007

2.The First Schedule of the Finance Act, No. 5 of 2005is hereby amended by the addition, at the end of that Scheduleof the following item:

5.The Inland Revenue Act, No. 10 of 2006 ( otherthan the provisions of Chapter XVI, XVII, XXI andsections 36 and 65). .

3.In the event of any inconsistency between theSinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shallprevail.
