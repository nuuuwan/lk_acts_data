AN ACT TO AMEND THE EXCISE (SPECIAL PROVISIONS)ACT, NO. 13 OF 1989

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Excise (SpecialProvisions) (Amendment) Act, No. 46 of 2007.

2.Section 5 of the Excise (Special Provisions) Act,No. 13 of 1989 is hereby amended, in sub-paragraph (ii)of paragraph (b) of subsection (2) of that section by thesubstitution for the words at the rate of ten per centumof the words at the rate of fifteen per centum.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between theSinhala and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shallprevail.
