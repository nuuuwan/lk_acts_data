AN ACT TO AMEND THE REGIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENTLEVY ACT, NO. 51 OF 2006

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Regional InfrastructureDevelopment Levy (Amendment) Act, No. 47 of 2007.

2. Section 2 of the Regional Infrastructure DevelopmentLevy Act, No. 51of 2006 is hereby amended, by thesubstitution for the words at the rate of two and halfper centum of the words at the rate of two and half percentum in respect of a motor vehicle of a cylinder capacitynot exceeding 1600, five per centum in respect of a motorvehicle of a cylinder capacity between 1600 and 2000 andseven and half per centum in respect of a motor vehicle of acylinder capacity exceeding 2000.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
