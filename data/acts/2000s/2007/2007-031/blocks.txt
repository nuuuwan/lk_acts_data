AN ACT TO AMEND THE JUDICATURE ACT, NO. 2 OF 1978

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Judicature (Amendment)Act, No. 31 of 2007 and shall come into operation on suchdate as the Minister may appoint by Order published in theGazette.

2.Section 4 of the Judicature Act, No. 2 of 1978 ishereby amended by the substitution for the words not morethan forty Judges. of the words not more than sixty Judges..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
