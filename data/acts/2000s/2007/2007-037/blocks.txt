AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 11 OF 2004

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1. This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment)Act, No. 37 of 2007.

2.Part II (Cellular Mobile Telephone Subscribers Levy)of the Finance Act, No. 11 of 2004 is hereby amended insection 14 thereof by the substitution for the words at therate of two and half per centum of the words at the rate often per centum.

3.In the event of an inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
