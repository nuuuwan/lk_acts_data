Appropriation (Amendment) Act, No. 43 of 2003

(Certified on 18th December, 2003]

L.D.—O. 153/34.
AN ACT TO AMEND THE APPROPRIATION AT, No. 31 oF 2002

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :-—

1. This Act may be cited as the Appropriation
(Amendment) Act, No. 43 of 2003.

2. Section 2 of the Appropriation Act, No. 31 of 2002 is
hereby amended in paragraph (b) of subsection (1) of that
section, by the substitution for the words “rupees three
hundred and sixteen thousand million”, of the words “rupees
three hundred and seventy-five thousand six hundred and
fifty seven million.”.

3. Inthe event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
