AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL HOUSING DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITYACT, NO. 17 OF 1979

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the National HousingDevelopment Authority (Amendment) Act, No. 32 of 2003.

2.Section 10 of the National Housing DevelopmentAuthority Act, No. 17 of 1979 is hereby repealed.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
