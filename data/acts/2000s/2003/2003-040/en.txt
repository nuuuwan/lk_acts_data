L. D. — O. 37/2000

AN ACT TO AMEND THE Coconut DEVELOPMENT
Act, No. 46 or 1971

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Coconut Development
(Amendment) Act, No. 40 of 2003.

2. Section 2 of the Coconut Development Act, No. 46 of
1971 is hereby amended by the repeal of paragraph (vii) of
subsection (1) thereof and the substitution therefor, of the
following paragraph :—

“(vii) fix the number of members of the Board, the number
so fixed being’not more than eleven;”

3. Inthe event of any inconsistency between the Sinhala
and Tamil texts of this Act the Sinhala text shall prevail.
