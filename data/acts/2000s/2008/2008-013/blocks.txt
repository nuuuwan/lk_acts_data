AN ACT TO AMEND THE BUTCHERS ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Butchers (Amendment)Act, No. 13 of 2008.

2.Section 18 of the Butchers Ordinance (Chapter 272)(hereinafter referred to as the principal enactment) is herebyamended by the repeal of subsection (1) of that section andthe substitution therefor of the following subsection :

(1) It shall not be lawful for any person (not alicensed butcher) to slaughter any cattle without apermit from the proper authority :

Provided that no pregnant or lactating cows shallbe slaughtered whether under the authority of apermit or not..

3.Section 20 of the principal enactment is herebyamended as follows :

(1)by the re-numbering of that section as subsection(1) thereof ; and

(2)by the addition immediately after the re-numberedsubsection (1) of the following new subsection :

(2) Any person who slaughters a pregnant cowor a lactating cow in contravention of the provisionsof subsection (1) of section 18, shall be guilty of an
