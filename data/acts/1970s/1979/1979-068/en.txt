Procedure (Amendment) Act, No. 68 of 1979.

2. Section 115 of the’ Code of Criminal Procedure Act,

No. 15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the “ principal -

enactment”) is hereby amended by the repeal of subsection
(3) of that section and the substitution therefor of the
following new subsection :—

(3) Subject to the provisions of the Criminal
Procedure (Syecial Provisions) Law, No. 15 of 1978, a
Magisirate shall not release on bail or ctherwice any person

provisions of the Criminal Procedure (Special Provisions)
Law, No. 15 of 1978, be released on bail if proceedings are
not instituted against him in a Magistrate’s Court or the High
Court before the expiration of a period of three months
from the date he surrendered to court or is arrested unless
