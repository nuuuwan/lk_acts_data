AN Acr PROVIDE FOR MATTERS IN CONNECTION WITH VERDICTS,
REASONS THEREFOR AND SENTENCES RELATING TO TRIALS
COMMENCED UNDER PROVISIONS OF LAW IN FORCE BETWEEN
January 1, 1974 anv Juty2, 1979 AND FoR Marrens
CONNECTED THEREWITH OR INCIDENTAL THERETO.

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Criminal Trials (Special
Provisions) Act, No. 66 of 1979.

2 (1) Where, under the provisions of any law which
was in force from January 1, 1974 to July 2, 1979, any
verdict, reasons for the verdict or sentence, was or were
required to be recorded within a period specified in such
law, the failure to record such verdict, reasons or sentence
within such period shall not be deemed to affect or to have
affected the validity of such verdict, reasons or sentence
unless such failure had occasioned a substantial miscarriage
of justice.

(2) The provisions of subsection (1) shall apply to all
matters pending in any Court, in appeal or otherwise.
