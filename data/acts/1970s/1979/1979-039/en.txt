L.D.—O. 125/78
An Act TO AMEND THE HousING AND TOWN IMPROVEMENT

ORDINANCE

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows :—

1. This Act may be cited as the Housing and Town Im-

provement (Amendment) Act, No. 39 of 1979.

2. The Schedule to the Housing and Town Improvement
Ordinance is hereby amended in rule 1 thereof, by the
substitution, for all the words and figures from “where a
building” to “from the building in question: ”, of the fol-
Jowing :—

“ where a building (not being a place of religious wor-
ship) is situated upon a street, no portion of the front
of the building shall intersect any of a series of imagi-
nary lines drawn across the street at an angle of 633°
with the plane of the lowest floor of the building, such
lines being drawn from the street alignment on the side
of the street which is the more remote from the building
in question: ”.
