AN ACT TO AMEND THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDUREACT, NO. 15 OF 1979

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows: -

1. This Act may be cited as the Code of CriminalProcedure (Amendment) Act, No. 38 of 2022.

2. Section 281 of the Code of Criminal Procedure Act,No. 15 of 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment) is hereby amended by the substitution for thewords, appears to the court to be under the age of eighteenyears, of the words is under the age of eighteen years,at the time of the commission of such offence by suchperson,.

3. Section 286 of the principal enactment is herebyamended by the substitution for the words and figures, Inthis section and in section 288 the expression of the wordsIn this section the expression.

4. Section 288 of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed and the following section is substituted therefor:

288. When any person has been sentencedunder section 53 of the Penal Code(Chapter 19), such person shall be detained inan institution established under any writtenlaw for the detention of persons under the ageof eighteen years, for a period specified in thesentence and subject to the provisions of suchwritten law..
