AN ACT TO AMEND THE APPROPRIATION ACT, NO. 30 OF 2021

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1.This Act may be cited as the Appropriation(Amendment) Act, No. 42 of 2022.

2.Section 2 of the Appropriation Act, No. 30 of 2021 asamended by Act, No. 21 of 2022 is hereby further amended inparagraph (b) of subsection (1) thereof, by the substitutionfor the words rupees three thousand eight hundred fourtyfour billion, of the words rupees four thousand five hundredseven billion.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
