AN ACT TO AMEND THE CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF TRANSPORT OFSRI LANKA (INCORPORATION) ACT, NO. 8 OF 2000

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows :

1.This Act may be cited as the Chartered Institute ofTransport of Sri Lanka (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act,No. 18 of 2023.

2.The long title to the Chartered Institute of Transportof Sri Lanka (Incorporation) Act, No. 8 of 2000, (hereinafterreferred to as the principal enactment) is hereby amendedby the substitution, for the words Chartered Institute ofTransport of Sri Lanka. of the words Chartered Institute ofLogistics and Transport of Sri Lanka..

3.(1) In the principal enactment and in any other writtenlaw there shall be substituted for the words CharteredInstitute of Transport of Sri Lanka wherever such wordsappear in the principal enactment and such other written lawof the words Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transportof Sri Lanka.

(2) Every reference to the Chartered Institute of Transportof Sri Lanka in any notice, notification, contract,communication or other document shall be read andconstrued as a reference to the Chartered Institute of Logisticsand Transport of Sri Lanka.

4.For the avoidance of doubts it is hereby declaredthat the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport of SriLanka shall for all purposes be deemed to be the successorto the Chartered Institute of Transport of Sri Lanka.
