AN ACT TO AMEND THE FINANCE ACT, NO. 35 OF 2018

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the Finance (Amendment)Act, No. 2 of 2020.

2.Section 36 of the Finance Act, No. 35 of 2018 is herebyamended in subsection (1) thereof, by the substitution for thewords and figures ending on December 31, 2021, of thewords and figures ending on December 31, 2019,.

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
