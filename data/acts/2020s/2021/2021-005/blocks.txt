AN ACT TO AMEND THE PENAL CODE ( CHAPTER 19 )

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:-

1.This Act may be cited as the Penal Code(Amendment) Act, No. 5 of 2021.

2.The word Ceylon, wherever it appears in the PenalCode (Chapter 19) (hereinafter referred to as the principalenactment) other than in sections 3, 18A, 120, 138, 158,159, 160, 225 and 256A thereof, shall be substituted with theword Sri Lanka.

3.The word Queen, wherever it appears in theprincipal enactment, other than in sections 19, 115 and 120thereof, shall be substituted with the word Republic.

4.Section 12 of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed and the following section is substituted therefor:-

12. The word Republic denotes theDemocratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka..

5.Section 13 of the principal enactment is herebyrepealed.
