L.D.O. 25/2019

AN ACT TO AMEND THE NATIONAL MINIMUM WAGE OF WORKERSACT, NO. 3 OF 2016

BE it enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic SocialistRepublic of Sri Lanka as follows:

1.This Act may be cited as the National MinimumWage of Workers (Amendment) Act, No. 16 of 2021.

2.Section 3 of the National Minimum Wage of WorkersAct, No. 3 of 2016 is hereby amended in subsection (1) thereofby the substitution for the words, shall be ten thousandrupees and the national minimum daily wage of a workershall be four hundred rupees of the words, shall be twelvethousand five hundred rupees and the national minimumdaily wage of a worker shall be five hundred rupees..

3.In the event of any inconsistency between the Sinhalaand Tamil texts of this Act, the Sinhala text shall prevail.
